Privacy Policy - Asprients Study Hub

Effective Date: October 5, 2025

Information We Collect +

  • Lecture Usage: Courses/lectures watched, notes downloaded, practice questions attempted.
  • Non-Personal Info: IP address, browser type, device info, site navigation, cookies, analytics data.
  • Behavioral Data: Study preferences, time spent per lecture, interaction with quizzes and notes.
How We Use Your Info +
  • Deliver and improve personalized study content, lectures, and quizzes.
  • Track progress and provide insights for CUET, CA Foundation, and Board exam preparation.
  • Respond to inquiries, support requests, or feedback.
  • Send updates, newsletters, and promotions (opt-out possible).
  • Analyze trends and improve study recommendations.
  • Prevent misuse or unauthorized access to accounts and study materials.
Cookies & Tracking +

We use cookies to save your login state, preferences, lecture progress, and site performance analytics. You can disable cookies, but some features like lecture progress tracking may not function properly.

Sharing & Disclosure +
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • Share info with service providers for hosting, analytics, or email notifications.
  • Disclose info to comply with legal obligations or protect rights.
  • Third-party services may collect info for ads or analytics.
Data Retention +

We retain user account and study progress data as long as the account is active. Inactive accounts may have data deleted after a period. Personal info is kept only as long as needed for legal or business purposes.

Data Security +

We use encryption, secure servers, and organizational measures to protect your account and study data. No system is 100% secure, so always keep your credentials private.

Analytics & Third-Party +

We may use Google Analytics or other services to track site usage. Third-party providers follow their own privacy policies. Data is anonymized whenever possible.

User Rights +
  • Access, update, or delete your account and study data.
  • Request copies of your data in machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing emails.
  • Opt-out of any data sharing where applicable.
International Data Transfer +

Your data may be transferred to servers outside your country to provide services globally. Transfers comply with applicable laws.
